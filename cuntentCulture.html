Indigenous medicine ( ayuruvedic )

Indigenous medicine is also a significant component of the culture and has even been said to be able to treat fatal illnesses like cancer. Many ayurvedic spas and clinics are now available for tourists visiting Sri Lanka, which is also thought to be one of the main tourist (recreational) attractions.

Sri Lankan Cuisine

Our mouthwatering, diverse food is the most important aspect of our culture (for all food lovers). The dishes may have some Indian influences, but they are nonetheless highly distinctive in their own right. Most of the fruits and vegetables that are used in cooking can only seldom be obtained in Sri Lanka. The distinctive flavor of Sri Lankan cuisine is integrated by a range of spices. In addition to being delicious, it is commonly accepted that the cuisine is prepared in a way that promotes a very healthy lifestyle. The versatility of the cuisine is its best feature.

Visual and Performance Arts

The fusion of cultures also includes music, drama, architecture, and other creative endeavors. The role of art in Sri Lankan history is crucial because it offers anthropologists and archaeologists a literal gold mine of information about Sri Lankan culture. From frescoes to castles, art itself documents history and culture, inspiring awe, admiration, and respect in viewers.
